Refresh the freight brand into a fuller logistics look. Headings pick up a DIN-flavored semi-condensed signage voice (`--font-display`: Barlow Semi Condensed) and technical text a documentary mono (`--font-mono`: IBM Plex Mono) — both degrade gracefully when the webfonts aren't loaded, which stays the app's job. The primary shifts to a livelier port-authority blue (#175490) and the accent deepens to a signal orange (#D0591C) that carries white text. Chrome gets a deeper navy rail with brighter active-nav highlights, elevation tokens turn navy-tinted in light mode (with explicit black-based dark counterparts), chart slots 2/4–8 become a cartographic palette (harbor teal, steel slate, crane red, sky, buoy amber, container green), surface neutrals cool toward steel with crisper borders, and the map tokens harmonize with the new primary hue.
